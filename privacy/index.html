Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Bathara Space ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our applications and services.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our applications, we may collect:

  • TikTok Account Information: When you authorize via TikTok Login Kit, we receive your TikTok user ID, username, and public profile data.
  • Content Data: Video content and captions that you choose to post or upload through our services.
  • Usage Data: Basic analytics about how you interact with our applications.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To facilitate posting content to your TikTok account
  • To improve our applications and user experience
  • To provide technical support

3. Data Storage & Security

We store your data securely and do not retain video content longer than necessary to complete the posting process. Access tokens are encrypted and stored with limited validity periods.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share data with TikTok as necessary to provide the core functionality of our applications.

5. Your Rights

You may revoke our access to your TikTok account at any time via your TikTok settings. You may also request deletion of your data by contacting us.

6. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, contact: batharaspace@proton.me

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
